## Current Interpretation

The images appear to be Sanborn-style fire insurance map excerpts for Oregon City, Oregon. They show the block bounded by Main Street, Water Street, 5th Street, and 6th Street, including block 194 and the Main Street frontage relevant to 503, 505, and 507 Main.

Visible labels include restaurants, billiards and cigars, drugs, grocery, lodgings, moving pictures, club/cleaning/confectionery uses, Oregon City Laundry, auto sales, machine shop, telephone office/shop, and retail paints.

The screenshots may represent different Sanborn editions, years, or revisions. Do not merge their details into one timeline until the exact source sheet, year, and page are confirmed.
