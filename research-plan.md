# Research Plan

This plan turns the archive's open questions into practical research tasks. Every new finding should be entered into the Evidence Register, Source Register, relevant building/business/person pages, and structured database files when appropriate.

## Priorities

1. Verify building dates and address histories for 503, 505, and 507 Main.
2. Build year-by-year occupant and business timelines.
3. Resolve named-person identities without merging uncertain people.
4. Confirm The Wheel / 505 Tavern / Thirsty Duck ownership sequence.
5. Confirm the Harding Building fire date, cause, and rebuild record.
6. Preserve and describe primary artifacts, especially matchbooks and matchcovers.

## City Directories and Telephone Books

Purpose: establish year-by-year occupants, business names, addresses, and people.

Tasks:

- Search Polk directories for 503 Main, 505 Main, and 507 Main.
- Search Oregon City telephone books for telephone 6106.
- Track entries for Kwality Cafe, New Kwality Cafe, Larry's Kwality Cafe, The Wheel, 505 Tavern, Harding Drug Store, Tony's Restaurant, and any bakery tenant.
- Track people: J. Jager, Larry, George A. Harding, Dr. Bailey, Ray Hansen, Ray Grisham, Dick, Sheila, Willie, Victor, Mitch Young, Mike Berman, and Scott Forvilly.
- Add each directory item as an evidence record with source citation and confidence.

Related leads: `RL-001`, `RL-003`

## Assessor, Tax, Permit, and Building Records

Purpose: verify construction dates, remodels, rear additions, ownership, and replacement buildings.

Tasks:

- Check assessor records for 503 Main, 505 Main, and 507 Main.
- Test whether 1919 for 503 Main is original construction, remodel, rebuild, or inherited record date.
- Determine when 505 Main and its rear portion were constructed.
- Find permit or assessor documentation for the 1968 replacement building at 507 Main.
- Add source URLs, scans, or file locations to the Source Register.

Related leads: `RL-002`, `RL-004`

## Deeds and Property Records

Purpose: establish legal ownership and building/property relationships.

Tasks:

- Pull deed chains for 503 Main, 505 Main, and 507 Main.
- Look for George A. Harding in 507 Main deed records.
- Look for ownership or lease evidence connected to The Wheel, 505 Tavern, and Harding Drug Store.
- Distinguish property ownership from business operation.

## Newspapers

Purpose: find public evidence for openings, ads, fires, ownership changes, and business names.

Tasks:

- Search for "Kwality Cafe," "New Kwality Cafe," "Larry's Kwality Cafe," "The Wheel," "The Wheel Tavern," "505 Tavern," "Thirsty Duck," "Harding Drug Store," and "Tony's Restaurant."
- Search for George A. Harding and Dr. Bailey.
- Search late 1967 and early 1968 newspapers for the Harding Building fire.
- Search for business ads that include street addresses.
- Save article citations and URLs in `evidence/source-register.md`.

## Sanborn Maps and Historic Maps

Purpose: confirm building footprints, use categories, and changes over time.

Tasks:

- Locate Sanborn maps covering Main Street in Oregon City.
- Use the archived map screenshots in `maps/images/` (see `maps/sanborn-main-water-5th-6th.md`) to recover the exact `orcity.org` source page, map year, sheet number, and edition.
- Compare 503, 505, and 507 Main across available years.
- Note building materials, uses, rear additions, and address numbering.
- Store map references under `maps/` and cite them in the Evidence Register.

## Artifacts

Purpose: preserve physical evidence and extract details from primary artifacts.

Tasks:

- Archive images or metadata for the New Kwality Cafe matchbook and Larry's Kwality Cafe matchcover.
- Look for manufacturer marks, typography clues, phone-number clues, and collector notes that may help date the artifacts.
- Search for menus, signs, business cards, photographs, bottles, prescription labels, and advertisements.
- Link every artifact to `A-###`, `E-###`, `S-###`, buildings, businesses, and people.

## Oral History

Purpose: preserve direct testimony and generate follow-up evidence leads.

Tasks:

- Follow up with Mitch Young using the questions in `oral-history/mitch-young.md`.
- Ask about The Wheel's legal name, address, prior owners, later owners, restaurant operation, employees, and surviving artifacts.
- Identify full names for Dick, Sheila, Willie, and Victor.

## Harding Building Fire

Purpose: resolve the late-1967 / early-1968 fire uncertainty.

Tasks:

- Search historic newspapers for the exact fire date.
- Check fire department records for incident reports.
- Check permit records for demolition, repair, or replacement construction.
- Determine whether Tony's Restaurant or a bakery was present at the time of the fire.
- Record official cause, damage description, and any names involved.

Related lead: `RL-004`

## Evidence Handling Rules

- Do not delete conflicting evidence.
- Do not renumber existing IDs.
- Record confidence for every evidence item.
- Preserve source URLs and local file paths.
- Distinguish primary documents, artifacts, direct testimony, family oral history, secondary sources, and community recollections.
- Update related pages whenever a new finding is added.
