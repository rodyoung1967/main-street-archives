# Archive Registers

This folder contains structured registers for cross-referencing the archive.

Registers are not narrative histories. They are lookup tables that help connect buildings, businesses, people, evidence, sources, media, ownership, and research work.

## Registers

- `address-register.md` — addresses and related buildings/businesses
- `ownership-timeline.md` — owner/stewardship chronology
- `business-timeline.md` — business-name and operation chronology
- `research-log.md` — research actions, searches, findings, and dead ends

## Rule

When a claim becomes important enough to appear in the narrative pages, it should also be linked back to the relevant register, evidence item, and source item where possible.
