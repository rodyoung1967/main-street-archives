# Tax Records

This folder is for tax statements, assessor materials, parcel records, tax cards, improvement records, and related transcriptions for 503 and 505 Main Street.

## Current Research Use

Tax and assessor records should be used to test:

- construction dates for 503 and 505 Main;
- whether the 1919 date for 503 reflects original construction, remodel, rebuild, or inherited record data;
- whether the rear portion of 505 was added later;
- whether 505 extends farther back than 503;
- whether 503 is wider than 505;
- parcel descriptions and legal boundaries between 503 and 505;
- assessed improvement changes over time;
- ownership clues for the Young, Grisham, and Wiitanen family connections.

## Handling Rule

Do not summarize tax statements generally without creating a specific evidence item or transcription note. Each tax record should eventually be linked to the Evidence Register and Source Register.
