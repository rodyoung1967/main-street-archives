## Known / Working Biography

Larry is associated with Larry's Kwality Cafe at 503 Main Street. The archive currently does not establish Larry's surname, formal role, operating dates, or relationship to Kwality Cafe / New Kwality Cafe.

The name comes from the business name printed on a Larry's Kwality Cafe matchcover. Until more evidence is found, "Larry" should be treated as an identified first name or business-name clue, not a complete person identification.
