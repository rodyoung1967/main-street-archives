## Known / Working History

The archive uses **The 505 / 505 Main Street business** as the preferred working label for the business context that Mitch Young directly confirmed he owned and operated at **505 Main Street** from **March 1991 to March 2004**.

This page exists to prevent that high-confidence 505 Main Street testimony from being incorrectly filed under the wrong location or automatically merged with **The Wheel**.

## Location Handling

- Correct confirmed location for Mitch Young's ownership/operation testimony: **505 Main Street, Oregon City, Oregon**.
- Do not treat Mitch Young's March 1991-March 2004 ownership/operation period as proof that the business was formally called **The Wheel**, **The Wheel Tavern**, or **505 Tavern** without documentary support.
- The Wheel remains a related but unresolved business-name/address question in the broader 503/505 Main context.

## Evidence Summary

- `E-014` - Project correction note: Mitch owned and operated the 505 Main Street business / The 505, not automatically The Wheel.
