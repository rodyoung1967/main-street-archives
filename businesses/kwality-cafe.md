## Known / Working History

Kwality Cafe is an early cafe/restaurant occupant associated with 503 Main Street. Current evidence points to at least three related names or phases:

- Kwality Cafe
- New Kwality Cafe
- Larry's Kwality Cafe

The exact sequence, dates, ownership continuity, and relationship between these names remain unresolved. These names should be preserved separately until documentary evidence confirms whether they were the same business, renamed versions of one business, or separate operators at the same address.

## Evidence Summary

`E-001` is a 2017 secondary source stating that 503 Main originally housed the Kwality Cafe. It also reports a 1919 construction date for 503 Main, but that date still needs primary confirmation.

`E-002` is a primary artifact: a New Kwality Cafe matchbook. It places New Kwality Cafe at 503 Main St., identifies J. Jager as proprietor, and includes the phrase "We Never Close."

`E-003` is a primary artifact: a Larry's Kwality Cafe matchcover. It places Larry's Kwality Cafe at 503 Main Street, Oregon City, lists telephone 6106, and advertises merchant lunches, steaks, and chops.
