## Known / Working History

Harding Drug Store is associated with George A. Harding and the original Harding Building at 507 Main Street.

The archive currently treats Harding Drug Store as a key business connected to 507 Main, but the exact operating dates, legal ownership, tenancy arrangement, and documentary source trail still need to be established.

## George A. Harding Connection

George A. Harding is associated with both Harding Drug Store and the Harding Building.

- Person record: `P-001`
- Building record: `B-003`
- Business record: `BUS-007`

The archive should not assume whether Harding owned the building, developed it, leased space in it, or operated the drug store there until primary documents support the relationship.

## Building Context

Harding Drug Store belongs to the original historic Harding Building story, not the 1968 replacement building unless later evidence proves continuity.

The original Harding Building was reportedly destroyed by fire around late 1967 or early 1968. That fire date remains unresolved and is tracked by `RL-004`.
