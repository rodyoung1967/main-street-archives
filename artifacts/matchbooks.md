## Overview

The archive currently includes two matchbook or matchcover artifacts connected to the Kwality Cafe history at 503 Main Street:

- `A-001` - New Kwality Cafe matchbook
- `A-002` - Larry's Kwality Cafe matchcover

These are primary artifacts and should be treated as stronger evidence than later summaries or recollections for the specific claims printed on them.

## A-001 - New Kwality Cafe Matchbook

The New Kwality Cafe matchbook places New Kwality Cafe at 503 Main St. and identifies J. Jager as proprietor. It also includes the phrase "We Never Close."

- Evidence record: `E-002`
- Source record: `S-002`

## A-002 - Larry's Kwality Cafe Matchcover

The Larry's Kwality Cafe matchcover places Larry's Kwality Cafe at 503 Main Street, Oregon City. It lists telephone 6106 and advertises merchant lunches, steaks, and chops.

- Evidence record: `E-003`
- Source record: `S-003`
- Related business: `BUS-003`
- Related person: `P-004`
- Related building: `B-001`

## Evidence Cautions

These artifacts strongly support the printed business names, address, proprietor/operator clues, and advertised details. They do not by themselves establish:

- Exact operating dates
- Whether Kwality Cafe, New Kwality Cafe, and Larry's Kwality Cafe were the same legal business
- Whether J. Jager and Larry were connected
- Whether there is a verified Morton, Washington connection
