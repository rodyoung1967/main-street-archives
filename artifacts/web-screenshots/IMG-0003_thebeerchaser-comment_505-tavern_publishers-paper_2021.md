## Description

Screenshot of comments on Thebeerchaser site discussing the 505 Tavern on Main Street. The visible comment by Chuck Mitchell says he celebrated his high draft number in 1971 at the 505 Tavern on Main St. after walking off Machine #4 at Publishers Paper, and mentions the rule: "no heads on the bar."

A reply by Thebeerchaser says he delivered the paper to the 505, described it as a great bar, and recalled a bartender greeting him with "Howdy Young Fella" while regulars, including many Publisher's workers after their shifts, would clap.
