## OQ-005 — 501 Main on the 1925 Sanborn Map
Status: Partially answered by `E-020` / `EV-SAN-1925-1950-001` and `E-021`.

The 1925 Sanborn sheet shows a separate `501` label at or near the 5th/Main corner, with `503` and `505` separately labeled north/up the Main frontage.

**Answered in part:** `E-021` / `IMG-0007` (1936) shows **three distinct roofs at 501, 503, and 505 Main**, confirming 501 existed as a separable structure in the aerial era. By `IMG-0009` (1944) the **501 Main footprint is gone**, and the east-side building **across from 500 Main** (Sanborn-labelled counterpart) is also absent — **removal window: 1936–1944**.

**Still open:** What business, ownership chain, and legal cause of removal (highway acquisition, demolition, merger into adjacent addresses, etc.) belong to 501 Main? See `evidence/aerial-5th-main-photocompare-series.md`.
