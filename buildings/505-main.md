# 505 Main Street

## Known / Working History

505 Main is associated with 505 Tavern, The Wheel, and later Thirsty Duck.

## Business Ownership / Operation

- Ray Hansen owned the business before Dick and Sheila.
- Willie, a heavyset bartender, worked under Ray Hansen and continued under Dick and Sheila.
- Dick and Sheila owned the business after Ray Hansen.
- Victor independently managed the restaurant during Ray Grisham's years and early Dick and Sheila years.
- Mitch Young owned and operated The Wheel from March 1991 to March 2004, confirmed directly by Mitch.
- Mike Berman was a later owner.
- Scott Forvilly purchased the business in 2017 and renamed/reopened it as Thirsty Duck.
