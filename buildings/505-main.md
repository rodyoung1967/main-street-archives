## Known / Working History

505 Main Street is associated with The Wheel, 505 Tavern, and later Thirsty Duck. The building is also tied to a partly unresolved ownership and operations sequence involving Ray Hansen, Ray Grisham, Dick, Sheila, Willie, Victor, Mitch Young, Mike Berman, and Scott Forvilly.

The exact relationship between The Wheel and the 503/505 Main address boundary still needs confirmation. The archive should continue to preserve 505 Main and 503 Main as separate building records while acknowledging that The Wheel may involve the combined 503/505 Main context.

## Business Ownership / Operation

- `E-005` - Ray Hansen owned the 505 business before Dick and Sheila. This is currently family/oral history and needs documentary confirmation.
- `E-006` - Willie, a heavyset bartender, worked under Ray Hansen and continued under Dick and Sheila. His full name is not yet known.
- Dick and Sheila owned or operated the business after Ray Hansen. Their full names and exact dates are not yet established in the archive.
- `E-007` - Victor independently managed the restaurant during Ray Grisham's years and early Dick and Sheila years. Victor's full name and exact role need confirmation.
- `E-004` / `OH-001` - Mitch Young owned and operated The Wheel from March 1991 to March 2004, confirmed directly by Mitch.
- `E-008` - Mike Berman was a later owner and sold the business in 2017.
- `E-008` - Scott Forvilly purchased the business in 2017 and reopened it as Thirsty Duck.

## Ray Hansen / Ray Grisham Issue

There is an unresolved Ray Hansen / Ray Grisham naming issue that should not be silently merged.

Current handling:

- `P-005` Ray Hansen is preserved as a separate person record.
- `P-006` Ray Grisham is preserved as a separate person record.
- Claims involving Ray Hansen and Ray Grisham should remain separate until primary evidence or direct testimony establishes whether they are different people, alternate names, or a memory/transcription conflict.
