# 503 Main Street

## Known / Working History

503 Main is associated with the Kwality Cafe and later The Wheel.

## Construction Date

A 2017 article states the building was constructed in 1919. This is supported by at least one later commercial listing, but still needs primary confirmation.

## Kwality Cafe Evidence

- New Kwality Cafe matchbook: 503 Main St., J. Jager, Prop., 'We Never Close.'
- Larry's Kwality Cafe matchbook: 503 Main Street, Oregon City, telephone 6106, merchant lunches, steaks, chops.
- 2017 article says the building originally housed the Kwality Cafe.

## The Wheel Naming Issue

The 2017 article refers to 'The Wheel Tavern.' This should be treated as unverified shorthand unless primary evidence appears. Current preferred wording: **The Wheel**, a bar and grill.
