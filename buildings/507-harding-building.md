# 507 Main Street / Harding Building

## Known / Working History

The original Harding Building was associated with George A. Harding and the Harding Drug Store.

## Documented Occupant

In 1909, Dr. Bailey operated a dental practice upstairs in the Harding Building.

## Fire and Rebuild

The original historic Harding Building was reportedly destroyed in a catastrophic fire around late 1967 or early 1968. The replacement commercial building was completed in 1968.
